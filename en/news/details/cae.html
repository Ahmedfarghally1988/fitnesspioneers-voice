Women's Nutrition (Breast Cancer and PCOS) Free Course in Pink October

2022/10/23 | 182 | 0

Description

On the occasion of Pink October, Fitness Pioneers Academy presents, with Dr. Hisham Gouda, a free course entitled (Women's Nutrition, Breast Cancer and Polycystic Ovary Syndrome)

Various topics deal with the subject comprehensively, starting with the definition of breast cancer, its causes and symptoms, how it is diagnosed, and how to protect yourself from it, God willing.

In this course, we will also learn about foods that fight breast cancer
Which foods increase the risk of developing it and what are the most famous myths about breast cancer

In addition to polycystic ovaries, symptoms, causes and factors that increase the risk of infection
What are its complications and how do you protect yourself from it, God willing

You will also learn about a diet that helps in the case of polycystic ovaries and some other natural methods to counter polycystic ovaries.
