My personal trainer experience
At first, frankly, I did not feel that I would benefit from the course because I read a lot about sports and exercises, but there are many things in the course that I knew of their importance only from the course, especially in physiological physical exertion and the method of evaluating trainees and sports anatomy. Literally, you can invent exercises if you understand the anatomy perfectly. And also in terms of nutrition and program creation.
In short, an experience that was more than excellent. I thank all the organizers of this course, from the coordinators to the course presenters. They were all excellent.
